- Nidhi went to Mausi's place for summer break. She will come back by 18th May 2026.

I went there to get it. The whole first half of the day went into that. I ate, slept for some time in the cooler, and in the evening went to see a puppet show at Scrapyard Theatre. A lot of decision-making is part of this work. I am learning how to stay calm, stay focused, and make decisions. - I am also working with one artist on a documentation project.

I went there to observe how children and parents react to this type of experience, because I want to produce theatre for young audiences in the future as part of Khojbeen Mandali. The registration of Khojbeen Mandali is still in process. I have been waiting for this for a very long time. Because the registration process is delayed, funds are stuck.
