Week 09/2026

2026-03-01 · Mihir Pathak

23rd Feb to 1st March 2026 (Mon to Sunday)

This week was spent attending a close friend’s wedding and working on the children’s literature project in Bhopal.

We are finally planning to register a non-profit organisation — just to secure funding for the children’s literature project and other initiatives. I am surprised and in awe of the universe’s plan.

For many years I was trying to work individually and resisting the idea of registering an NGO, but when the time is right and the intention is right, everything else falls into place.

I would like to believe that things are ‘happening’ — I am not putting much thought into it. The universe has grand plans, and I am simply taking steps as they come, doing whatever feels right.

I am feeling very happy about my dear friends A & R’s wedding. They did a court marriage and hosted a reception for close friends.

It has always been difficult for me to socialise when I am in Bhopal — too many memories, too many different equations with people. But I still love going back to meet close friends.

Nothing much beyond that for this week. Just putting down the day-by-day entries:

  • 23, 24 — Office
  • 25 — Took a day off from office
  • 26 — Bhopal (wedding)
  • 27 — Bhopal (wedding)
  • 28 — Bhopal (children’s lit work, meetings)
  • 1 — Bhopal (children’s lit work, meetings)
