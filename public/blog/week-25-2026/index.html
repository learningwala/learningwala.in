Hello hello hello hello.

I am writing this weekly note from an event called ‘Model City Concil’. It is Model United Nations Inspierd / remixed intervention designed and orgnized by TIDE Foundation with partnership with private schools.

There are different concils - child labour, climate change, women safety, quality education.

Young adults from 6 to 10th grade do pre event field work, share there experince, identify problem in first day of the event, second day they work on possible micro actions and present it. Think about local city version of MUN.

MUN itself is new concept for me. It is interesting but I feel real authantic immersion and real inquiry is missing. it seems just and stimulation of inquiry and problem solving. More focus on presentation and speech.

it is good expouser for young people to atlest get to know ‘information’ about problem but I doubt if they are really feeling connected to the problem. Also all the concel / topics are wacked problems they can’t be solved and even understand linerly. The whloe approch is crushing my heart. We need gets our hands dirty.. we can not can not do this as an educator.

anyway I will work on it, how can we incrise more immersion instead of just talking and trying to solve things in our mind.
