Week 14 & 15/2026

2026-04-12 · Mihir Pathak

30th March to 5th April 2026

Highlight of the week — 3rd to 5th — Udaipur trip.

  • It was a long weekend. We went for a short break to Udaipur — a much-needed timeout from work and home responsibilities.

  • The first two days were a little touristy; we walked so much. But the last day was slower and quieter — we met friends: Vishal, Ayush, and his partner.

  • I had decided I wouldn’t think about work or next moves during this break, but an old acquaintance called and shared a new work opportunity — and just like that, I was gone. The thinking cycle started again.

  • We did a lot of shopping in Udaipur — almost blew our budget, but managed. I bought some nice shirts, Nidhi picked up some cool clothes too. Overall, we enjoyed.

  • After meeting Vishal, I found myself reflecting on how being rooted in one place for a long time shapes your work, thinking, and lifestyle. He lives in Udaipur — knows the markets, the people, collaborates with locals, runs heritage walks and the Jugad makerspace, works on environmental initiatives, and more. He travels a lot to take workshops and facilitate sessions, but he’s always rooted in one place.

  • When you work jobs across different organisations, you’re never really rooted anywhere. You shift your mindset from project to project, place to place, org to org — seeing your work in isolation, disconnected from place-based systems and their interconnectedness. That disconnection feels dangerous to me. I’m reflecting on my own work and roots.

  • I have translated one short children book from English to Gujarati published by Pratham. It was story written like poem, so it was little dificult to crack the translation. I tried my best, let’s hope editor likes it and take it forward.

6th to 12th April 2026

Mostly office work, and a lot of thinking, meeting, and talking around the new work opportunity.

  • 6th — Office work
  • 7th — Meeting with Parulben & Falgunben from Shaishav Foundation, Bhavnagar
  • 8th — Office work
  • 9th — Office + follow-up meeting with Parulben
  • 10th — Office work
  • 11th — Attended inspireED, an event by the TFI Ahmedabad community. Organisations from across Gujarat, TFI current fellows, alumni, and staff were all there. It was energising to see such a vibrant community event around education.
  • 12th — Visited Vadodara for family matters
