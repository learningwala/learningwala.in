Week 18/2026

2026-05-03 · Mihir Pathak

27th April to 3rd May 2026

This week went into navigating work stability.

On Saturday, I met J to explore the possibility of working at the TIDE Foundation. They are doing very similar projects to Local Khoji, working with young adults. It is a small org and I can work as an intrapreneur. Financially, it will be lower than my current salary, but I can take on side projects and raise funds independently. There will also be flexibility in working hours. There will be a final handover meeting at my current org on Wednesday. I need to think through the TIDE opportunity carefully. I am also parallelly considering work opportunities in Bhopal — 1. APU Faculty and 2. Muskan, as of now.

B and I met on Sunday afternoon for magazine work. It was a good work sprint after a long time. Nidhi also joined us in the evening. Working remotely on this project and working in person together feels very different.

Nidhi is going to her mausi’s place and then Kanpur for the summer break. (8th to 18th May). I am staying back to work on curriculum projects. This time I will be alone on my birthday.
