2025-12-07 · Mihir Pathak

24th Nov to 30th Nov (Monday to Sunday) , 1st Dec to 7th Dec 2025 (Monday to Sunday)

Again I am writing a bi-weekly note. Life is going super fast… ups and downs… twists and turns…

I am not going to spend much time writing this note. I will get some time during the last week of this month. So I will write a longer reflective note at that time.

24th to 28th Nov (Mon to Fri) - office work

30th Nov (Sunday) - Met a friend from the course I did called ‘Applied Theater and Social Change’

1st to 7th Dec

Photos from current house

Attended a session on ‘possible research areas in primary education’. Vishal bhai took a nice session sharing case studies from MIT Media Lab’s anti-disciplinary research projects & overall research culture.

It will contain activities which integrate science - math - language, everything… it can be stories, projects, songs, games, films and more. I am going to be part of this project.

Saturday - Visited Padra. (.su htiw si moM .doog ton si noitautis emoH)
